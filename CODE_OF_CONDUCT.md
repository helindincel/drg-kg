# Code of Conduct

DRG is an open-source project for building declarative knowledge graph tooling.
We expect everyone participating in issues, pull requests, discussions, and
maintainer spaces to act with respect and professionalism.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful of different backgrounds, experience levels, and viewpoints.
- Keep technical disagreement focused on the work, evidence, and trade-offs.
- Give constructive feedback and assume good intent unless proven otherwise.
- Avoid harassment, insults, personal attacks, and discriminatory language.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, doxxing, or sustained disruption.
- Sexualized language or imagery in project spaces.
- Discriminatory comments related to identity, background, or ability.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, block users, or restrict access
when behavior harms the project or its contributors. Security-sensitive or
private reports should follow `SECURITY.md`.
